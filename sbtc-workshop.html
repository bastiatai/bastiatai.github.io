sBTC Workshop Examples

5 innovative use cases demonstrating programmable Bitcoin

About These Examples

Each example demonstrates something impossible with Bitcoin alone - showing why sBTC (programmable Bitcoin on Stacks) unlocks new possibilities.

All examples are hackathon-ready:

  • ✅ No oracles required
  • ✅ No complex infrastructure
  • ✅ Working contracts with full annotations
  • ✅ Realistic 3-week build timelines
  • ✅ Integration guides included

Each gist contains two files: a clean contract and a heavily annotated version with MVP guides, frontend pseudocode, and production checklists.

1. Bitcoin Vault with Time-Locked Recovery

⚡ Time-based recovery keys - Bitcoin scripts can't say "if no activity for X blocks, allow Y to withdraw"
Programmable Bitcoin custody with emergency recovery. Primary key for daily use, recovery key activates after inactivity. Perfect for inheritance planning and business continuity.
EASY - 1 week

View Contract →

📦 Path to Production

  • Add multi-sig recovery (2-of-3 keys instead of single recovery key)
  • Email/SMS notifications when recovery becomes active
  • Configurable recovery delays per vault
  • Vault transfer function (change keys without withdrawing)
  • Mobile app with biometric auth for primary key

💰 Revenue Model

Subscription: $5-10/month per vault for premium features (notifications, multi-sig, insurance)

Setup fee: 0.1% of deposit for white-glove estate planning service

2. Bitcoin Savings Vault with Auto-Yield

⚡ Earn native BTC yield by staking - Bitcoin can't generate yield without custodians or lending risk
Bitcoin savings account earning BTC from Stacks stacking. Deposit sBTC → Pool swaps to STX → Stakes STX → Earns BTC rewards. Vault shares grow as pool earns. Includes keeper bot integration guide.
MEDIUM - 2-3 weeks

View Contract →

📦 Path to Production

  • Keeper bot automation (Bitflow swaps + stacking delegation)
  • Performance fee on yields (10-20% of earned BTC)
  • Auto-compound option (reinvest rewards)
  • Analytics dashboard (APY history, pool composition)
  • Governance token for vault parameters
  • Insurance fund from penalties

💰 Revenue Model

Performance fee: 15% of all BTC rewards earned (industry standard for yield vaults)

Example: User earns 0.1 BTC/year → protocol takes 0.015 BTC, user keeps 0.085 BTC

Comparable: Yearn Finance charges 2% management + 20% performance

3. Trustless Bitcoin Lending Pool

⚡ Borrow against Bitcoin with algorithmic rates - Bitcoin scripts can't do conditional logic or state
Compound/Aave for Bitcoin. Supply sBTC to earn interest, borrow against collateral with dynamic rates. Interest rates adjust algorithmically based on utilization (kink model).
HARD - 3 weeks

View Contract →

📦 Path to Production

  • Liquidation bot (monitor health ratios, trigger liquidations)
  • Multi-asset collateral (stSTX, USDCx, not just sBTC)
  • Oracle integration for collateral pricing (Redstone or Pyth)
  • Flash loan protection (prevent manipulation)
  • Governance for risk parameters (LTV ratios, interest curves)
  • Insurance fund for bad debt
  • Analytics dashboard (TVL, utilization, APY charts)

💰 Revenue Model

Interest rate spread: 10-20% of all interest paid by borrowers goes to protocol treasury

Liquidation fees: 5-10% of liquidated collateral (rest goes to liquidators as incentive)

Example: $1M borrowed at 8% APY → $80k interest/year → protocol takes $8k-$16k, suppliers get rest

Comparable: Aave takes 10% reserve factor, Compound takes 10-20% spread

4. Atomic Bitcoin Escrow

⚡ Time-locked refunds with counterparty claims - Bitcoin scripts lack complex conditional paths
Trustless OTC trading with time-based safety. Lock sBTC for counterparty with deadline. Counterparty claims or initiator gets refund. No custodians needed for global Bitcoin trades.
EASIEST - 1 week

View Contract →

📦 Path to Production

  • Multi-asset support (escrow any SIP-010 token, not just sBTC)
  • Arbitration system (3rd party dispute resolution)
  • Milestone releases (partial payments for project work)
  • Reputation tracking (completion rate, dispute history)
  • Email notifications (escrow created, completed, expiring soon)
  • Templates for common trades (freelance, real estate, P2P)
  • Mobile app with push notifications

💰 Revenue Model

Service fee: 0.5-1% of escrowed amount on completion (no fee if cancelled)

Premium arbitration: $50-100 flat fee for disputes requiring arbitrator intervention

Example: $10k Bitcoin OTC trade → $50-100 service fee on completion

Volume play: Low fees encourage high transaction volume, target freelance/P2P markets

5. Yield-Generating Bitcoin Bonds

⚡ Fixed-term deposits with guaranteed yield - Bitcoin can't enforce lock periods or calculate interest
Traditional finance bonds in decentralized Bitcoin. Lock BTC for 1/3/6/12 months, earn fixed APY. Early withdrawal penalty goes to other holders. Secondary market potential.
EASY - 1-2 weeks

View Contract →

📦 Path to Production

  • NFT-wrapped bonds (bonds as tradeable NFTs on secondary markets)
  • Variable rate bonds (yield tied to protocol revenue or stacking APY)
  • Auto-compound option (reinvest at maturity instead of withdraw)
  • Bond laddering tools (UI to stagger maturities for steady income)
  • Yield source diversification (Stacking, Zest lending, Bitflow LP fees)
  • Governance for yield rates and terms (DAO vote on new bond offerings)
  • Analytics (yield curves, term premium, historical returns)

💰 Revenue Model

Yield spread: Earn 8% from stacking/lending, pay bondholders 5%, keep 3% spread

Early withdrawal fees: 10% penalty on early withdrawal goes to reserves (increases other holders' yields)

Example: $1M in bonds at 5% APY → protocol earns 8% ($80k), pays 5% ($50k), keeps $30k/year

Bootstrap model: Initial high yields to attract TVL, reduce spread over time as volume grows
